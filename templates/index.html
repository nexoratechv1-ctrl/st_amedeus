Our History

St. Joseph's Catholic School, Moshi, Tanzania, was established in 1985 under the Catholic Diocese of Moshi. With a mission to provide accessible quality education, we have grown into a center of academic excellence, producing thousands of disciplined, God-fearing graduates serving the nation and world.
